About

I’m Rachel Soh, and I’m a highly enthusiastic student currently finishing my final year of BSc in Actuarial Science at the London School of Economics and Political Science. My favourite fields lie in equity markets analytics and Artificial Intelligence stock trading.

I’ve been coding for the last five years but only recently decided to kickstart my Data Science journey. I’ve also worked on projects including artificial intelligence, databases, financial markets, and interactive choice games. I have a fair share of experience with Python, R, SQLite, Turbo Pascal, and Donacode (and a little bit of HTML after setting up this site).
